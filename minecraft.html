Minecraft Project

We never really had a designated "minecraft project". At some point we had to pick a company to create marketing for, and I chose the company behind minecraft. I made a youtube thumbnail showing how friendly pillagers are, and finally a moodboard that showcases minecraft's adventurous feel. Overall, I'm pretty happy with how it turned out.

Artist Statement

I’m pretty sure this was my favorite project yet. First off, let's start off with the mood board. Most of my images are cinematic texture packed versions of minecraft, to make it feel more authentic. I also included my textured image around the title of the whole thing, “Minecraft”. In the background, the gutters are made of the nether dimension, showing how the game can switch from peaceful to hardcore in a heartbeat. I also have the words “A world where anything is possible”, which I’m pretty sure came from the trailer of the game. I also have a color palette consisting of green, brown, grey, and blue, symbolizing green, dirt, stone and water. Alright, let's mention the thumbnail. It's Mr Zeng being robbed by pillagers. I also included the official minecraft title card, because I got inspired by some nostalgic minecraft thumbnails that I researched. The fact that we were able to choose essentially whatever we wanted made me have a blast with his project, so yay.
