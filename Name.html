Name Project

For this project, we had to take pictures of random objects around school and assemble them into our names. Also, we had to talk about the history behind our names. During this project, I learned a bit about myself and my great great grandfather, John Harrison from england. I also took some pretty clever photos, such as a crutch for the letter A. It was a pretty cool project.

Artist Statement

I’m named after the last name of my Mom’s side, Harrison. The first Harrison that we remember is John Harrison, my great great grandfather. It’s an english name, and indeed John came from England, but that’s pretty much all I know about him. Here’s a fun fact about my name, almost everyone I know says it wrong (at least in brooklyn). Most people say Hair-i-son when it’s really Ha-ri-son. I think I tried to tell people that my name was actually Ha-ri-son for 3 years up until 3rd grade where I just gave up.
