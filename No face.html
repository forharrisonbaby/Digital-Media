No Face Project

This project cemented the end of our photoshop arc. We had to use all of our photoshop skills in one final piece that represented us with "no face". I decided to use a wardrobe, with each shelf peering into a different dimension. For example, there's a cowboy dimension with nothing but a boot and a leg, showcasing how bootleg this really was. But hey, I used every technique in the book to create it, and I'm proud of the final product.

Artist Statement

I originally only took a photo of myself and the open closet of the library, so I knew I had to get creative with this one. I imagined that each shelf of the closet would represent a slice of who I am, so I set off to make that a reality. In the end, each shelf ended up being a portal to another dimension, which I think represents the intricacies of who I am.
